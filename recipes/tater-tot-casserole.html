Tater Tot Casserole

Home

Description

Craving a super easy and delicious dinner that everyone at your table will devour? This Tater Tot casserole is exactly what you need! It's a truly comforting dish that comes together with just four simple ingredients. You can't beat that for a quick weeknight meal!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef: Grab about 1 to 1.5 pounds of ground beef. You'll cook this until it's nice and brown and crumbly.
  • Cream of Mushroom Soup: You'll want one standard can (around 10.5 ounces) of condensed cream of mushroom soup. This is your secret weapon for richness and creaminess!
  • Seasonings: Just the basics here! Have some salt (maybe 1/2 to 1 teaspoon, or to taste) and black pepper (a generous pinch or two) ready to go.
  • Tater Tots: Of course, the stars of the show! You'll need about one 32-ounce bag of frozen tater tots.
  • Cheddar Cheese: For the best melty goodness, get about 1.5 to 2 cups of shredded Cheddar cheese. Trust us, shredding your own makes a difference!

Steps

  1. First things first, get all your ingredients ready to go. While you’re doing that, go ahead and preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Next, grab a large skillet and heat it over medium-high. Toss in your ground beef and cook it up, stirring occasionally, until it’s all browned and crumbly – that should take about 7 to 10 minutes. Once it's cooked, stir in that condensed soup, then add your salt and black pepper.
  3. Now, transfer that yummy beef mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer your tater tots evenly on top, and then sprinkle generously with that delicious Cheddar cheese.
  4. Pop your casserole into the preheated oven and bake for about 30 to 45 minutes. You’ll know it’s ready when those tater tots are beautifully golden brown and piping hot!
  5. Finally, serve it up and enjoy every comforting bite!
